Featured Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Advanced | Duration: Full Day

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on the legendary "Teeth of the Devil" rapids. This challenging full-day expedition features Class IV-V rapids, dramatic drops, and heart-pounding whitewater action. Perfect for experienced rafters seeking an extreme adventure through stunning canyon landscapes.

  • Class IV-V Rapids
  • 8-10 hours on the river
  • Lunch included
  • Minimum age: 16 years

Big Mallard Adventure

Difficulty: Intermediate | Duration: Half Day

Navigate through exciting Class II-III rapids on this popular half-day trip. The Big Mallard section offers the perfect balance of thrilling whitewater and scenic beauty, making it ideal for families and first-time rafters looking for an authentic rafting experience without extreme difficulty.

  • Class II-III Rapids
  • 4-5 hours on the river
  • Snacks provided
  • Minimum age: 8 years

Family Float Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: Half Day

Perfect for families and beginners, this gentle float trip combines calm waters with occasional mild rapids. Enjoy wildlife viewing, swimming opportunities, and breathtaking scenery while learning basic rafting techniques from our expert guides in a relaxed, fun environment.

  • Class I-II Rapids
  • 3-4 hours on the river
  • Refreshments included
  • Minimum age: 5 years
